Privacy Policy

Our authentication is based on Discord, so we store very little of your data.


Data Logging and Usage

To ensure this software operates reliably and securely, we may log and store certain information. We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties. All data handling practices comply with the GDPR and relevant government regulations.

  • Information returned if you connect your Discord account (username, discord ID, and any other OAuth scopes).
  • Activity logs detailing your interactions on the site (sign up date).
  • Attributes tied to your account on our platform (Roblox id).
  • Any scripts executed
  • Roblox profile pictures (cached).

We respect your privacy. If you wish to delete all of your data from our systems, please contact us at removal@nett.wtf.
